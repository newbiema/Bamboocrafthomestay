FAQ

Common booking questions.

How do I book a room?

Tap any Book via WhatsApp button, send your dates, guest count, and room preference. The team will confirm availability and price.

Can I book tours without staying at the homestay?

Yes. Private tours, transport, and custom Lombok itineraries can be arranged for outside guests.

What payment methods are accepted?

Payment options can be confirmed by WhatsApp. Bank transfer and cash on arrival may be available depending on booking type.

Is airport pickup available?

Yes. Airport pickup from Lombok International Airport can be added to room or tour bookings.

Can the itinerary be changed?

Yes. Private tours can be adjusted for weather, travel pace, family needs, and preferred stops.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms depend on room dates and tour type. Ask for the current policy before confirming your booking.

Is accommodation included in tour packages?

Stay-and-tour packages can include accommodation. Day tours are usually quoted separately from rooms.

Do you support families and honeymoon trips?

Yes. Rooms, transport, and tour pacing can be arranged for families, couples, honeymoon guests, and small groups.
